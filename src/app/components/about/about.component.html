TEAM KILIMANJARO

As a team, we are not only trying to solve daily problems with technology but also to bring a positive impact to the community through our products, to address social awareness and environmental issues.


We are 6 multinational members with different backgrounds in design, visual arts, finance and software development.


Our web app would collate all the resources needed to help users be fully zero wasters. It will have a search engine to find zero waste stores nearby, information on where specific items are available, locations on where to recycle/repurpose items that are no longer needed, and tips on how to transition to a zero waste lifestyle.
